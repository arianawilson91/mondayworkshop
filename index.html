Run of show — 90 minutes

Times assume a 90-minute session. Slide the whole thing earlier or later; the proportions are what matter. The mock walkthrough video is your Wi-Fi-died backup for parts 1–2.

0:00–0:10
Welcome + why Claude

Intros. Each owner says their business name and their #1 time-sink — write these down, you'll reuse them in Discovery.

0:10–0:25
Part 1 — Everyone gets Claude Pro

Play or present the walkthrough (slides 2–8). Everyone signs up on their own laptop. Payment on personal devices only, never the projector.

0:25–0:35
Shared first prompt

Whole room runs the same prompt: "I run a small business. Give me 3 ways I could use you every week, with one example prompt for each."

0:35–0:55
Part 2 — Meet Cowork

Walkthrough slides 9–14, then live demo with the Brand Content practice folder. One volunteer's laptop on the projector (not the payment screen!).

0:55–1:25
Client discovery + playbooks ← THIS TOOL

Sit with each business (or pair them up). Run the Discovery tab together (~5 min each), then open their Playbook and have them run tailored prompt #1 live before you move to the next table.

1:25–1:30
Wrap-up + homework

Wrap-up tab on the projector. Everyone leaves with one homework task tied to their own playbook.

What everyone did today
  • Set up a Claude Pro account
  • Ran a first real prompt
  • Watched Cowork do a real task with real files
  • Got a personalized playbook built from your own business
Homework this week — one thing

Run one Cowork task before Friday — either the homework from your playbook, or any task you pick from the Cowork Tasks menu. It takes one folder and ten minutes. Reply to the follow-up email with what it made for you — the good and the weird.

Rules of thumb to leave with
  • Talk to Claude like a smart coworker, not a search engine
  • Give it your real context — your menu, your prices, your customers
  • Only give Cowork the folder it needs, and review before you send or post
  • If the first answer is off, say why — the second one is usually right
